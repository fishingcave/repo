## 〈懺悔文〉：
### 往昔所造諸惡業，
皆由無始貪瞋癡，
從身語意之所生，
一切我今皆懺悔。
#### 這段懺悔文是出自《華
嚴經》最後一卷〈普賢菩薩
行願品〉。

#### All the evil karma that I have created in the past,
Is all due to beginningless greed, hatred, and delusion,
Generated in body, speech, and thought,
I now repent and reform of it all.
